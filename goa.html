Famous Places in Goa

The
Pearl
of
the
Orient

Baga Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for nightlife, shacks, and water sports.

Calangute Beach

The largest beach in North Goa, often called the "Queen of Beaches".

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage site famous for housing the relics of St. Francis Xavier.

Se Cathedral

One of the largest churches in Asia, dedicated to St. Catherine of Alexandria.

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort overlooking the Arabian Sea, with a lighthouse.

Dudhsagar Waterfalls

Majestic four-tiered waterfall located on the Mandovi River, surrounded by lush forests.
