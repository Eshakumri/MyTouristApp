Famous Places in Kolkata

The

Victoria Memorial

A grand marble building dedicated to Queen Victoria, surrounded by lush gardens.

Howrah Bridge

An engineering marvel and iconic cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River.

Dakshineswar Kali Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Goddess Kali, located on the banks of the Hooghly River.

Indian Museum

The oldest and largest museum in India, showcasing rare artifacts and collections.

Marble Palace

A 19th-century mansion known for its neoclassical architecture and art collections.

St. Paul’s Cathedral

A beautiful Anglican cathedral with Gothic architecture, a serene spiritual landmark.
