Famous Places in Agra

The
City
of
the
Taj

Taj Mahal

The crown jewel of Mughal architecture, built by Shah Jahan in memory of Mumtaz Mahal.

Agra Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this red sandstone fort was the main residence of Mughal emperors.

Mehtab Bagh

A beautiful garden across the Yamuna River, offering stunning views of the Taj Mahal at sunset.

Itimad-ud-Daulah’s Tomb

Often called the “Baby Taj,” this tomb is known for its intricate marble inlay work.

Akbar’s Tomb

Located in Sikandra, this mausoleum reflects Mughal architecture and grandeur.

Fatehpur Sikri

The former Mughal capital built by Akbar, famous for Buland Darwaza and Jama Masjid.
