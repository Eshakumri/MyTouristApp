Famous Places in Chennai

The
Gateway
to
South
India

Marina Beach

One of the longest urban beaches in the world, perfect for sunrise walks and local street food.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

A stunning Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its colorful gopuram.

Fort St. George

The first British fortress in India, housing a museum with colonial artifacts.

San Thome Basilica

A Roman Catholic basilica built over the tomb of Saint Thomas the Apostle.

Guindy National Park

A unique wildlife sanctuary within the city, home to deer, jackals, and rich birdlife.

Valluvar Kottam

A monument dedicated to the Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar, featuring a huge stone chariot and inscriptions.
